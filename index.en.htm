The RSS (Rich Site Sumary) format is a standard for distributing news headlines over the Web. The "Projeto RSSficado" goal is to promote the format around Brazil, colecting RSS links or developing HTML parsers to convert portuguese blogs and new's sites to this format. You can see the list of "rssifyed's" sites at portuguese page.
